Apple Fall Spritz

Serving 5

Hard cider replaces prosecco in this fall-flavor spritz. To temper the bitterness, top with more club soda.

Ingredients

  • ice as needed
  • 2 apple slices
  • 1 fresh sage leaf
  • 3 ounces dry hard apple cider
  • 2 ounces Aperol
  • splash of club soda

Preparation

  1. Fill a glass with ice. Nestle apple and sage in glass. Pour hard cider and Aperol into glass, then top with club soda.
  2. Enjoy!
